About Me

I have recently completed a Full Stack Web Development Course at Jefferson State Community College in Hoover, AL. In class, we had a brief overview of the realm of full stack including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and MySQL. It was a very rewarding course for a person with no prior experience in programming. I am looking forward to continued study in this career path and have enrolled in several online classes. As my knowledge base continues to grow, I hope to be able to enter the exciting field of programming one day.
